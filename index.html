Wish You Were Here

Get a glimpse of what's happening at your favorite Florida theme parks with Wish You Were Here, the happiest app on the web!

Just enter your zip code or choose an attraction below to compare weather and ride wait times quickly and conveniently.

Please note that this site relies on information drawn from outside sources that might not be readily available during your visit. Before reporting an error, please try making your request a second time.

If a weather report or wait time is consistently broken, contact the administrator of the site.
